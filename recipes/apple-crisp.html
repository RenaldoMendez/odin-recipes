Apple Crisp

Description

Tender apples kissed with maple syrup and covered in a rich, crunchy topping. Very easy, and tastes great!!

Reviewers like to use Granny Smith or Gala apples and use pure maple syrup to coat the apples — and use more or less maple syrup depending on how sweet your apples are.

  • Prep: 20 mins

  • Cook: 35 mins

  • Total: 55 mins

  • Servings: 8

  • Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 5 medium (2-3/4" dia) (approx 3 per lb)s apples - peeled, cored, and sliced

  • ¾ cup maple syrup

  • ½ cup all-purpose flour

  • ½ cup rolled oats

  • ½ cup brown sugar

  • 1 pinch salt

  • ½ cup butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Place apples in an 8x8 inch baking dish. Toss apples with syrup. In a separate bowl, mix together flour, oats, sugar, and salt. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle mixture evenly over apples.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 minutes, until topping is golden brown. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Apple Crisp

Tender apples kissed with maple syrup and covered in a rich, crunchy topping. Very easy, and tastes great!!

Reviewers like to use Granny Smith or Gala apples and use pure maple syrup to coat the apples — and use more or less maple syrup depending on how sweet your apples are.

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 35 mins
  • Total: 55 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 servings
Ingredients
  • 5 medium (2-3/4" dia) (approx 3 per lb)s apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
  • ¾ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ½ cup butter, softened
